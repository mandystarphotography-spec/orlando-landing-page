The Three Worries I Hear Most

And how we get past them.

one

"What about the Florida weather?"

Heat, afternoon storms, harsh midday light. I know these conditions intimately. We time your day around golden hour, plan for clouds, and choose venues that photograph beautifully in your season. Rain plans included.

two

"Will an outdoor wedding feel less special?"

Outdoor is not the budget version of a ballroom. It is its own thing, and it deserves a photographer who takes it as seriously as any grand venue. Outdoor weddings are not one category among many. They are the only category I shoot.

three

"Will the photographer disappear after dinner?"

I am part of your day. Not a vendor who clocks out when the dancing starts. I dance, I celebrate, I am there for the moments that happen after the formal photos are done. That is when the real ones happen.
